This is a personal wiki (beta version) of mathematical knowledge: short entries on
definitions, constructions, and intuitions, cross-linked across topics.

Unlike the [Notes](/notes/) section — which collects longer, expository
write-ups — each wiki entry is meant to be **atomic**: one concept, one
page, read in a few minutes, with explicit links to neighbouring concepts.

The [graph view](/wiki/graph/) shows how entries connect.
